The Horticulture of Bonsai

Understanding what horticulture is and how it is so important to sustaining your Bonsai tree, is probably the most important part of the gardening process. What is horticulture though and what makes it so important?

Horticulture is basically the science of growing and sustaining healthy plants. Horticulture can be very vast and complex, but having even a simple understanding of basic horticulture principles can make all the difference in taking care of your Bonsai tree.

Nebari is a key component of a Bonsai tree. Nebari references the roots of the tree. As the Bonsai tree grows, cultivating the roots is mandatory. Nebari is a much needed requirement because it gives the tree the proper visual balance, but also big, strong roots are vital to a strong and healthy Bonsai.
