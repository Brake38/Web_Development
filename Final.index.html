The Beautiful World of Bonsai!

Welcome to Brake Bonsai! We believe that the splendor and pure joy of caring for and nurturing a Bonsai tree is full of relaxation and tranquility. Though the journey from a seedling to a fully grown tree is long and winding, there are many virtues such as patience, kindness, and responsibility which will be bestowed upon those who fully divulge themselves in the art and practice of Bonsai.

We here at Brake Bonsai understand that experience is the greatest teacher of all and exposing yourself to new challenges will only increase your knowledge of when, why, and how when tending to your Bonsai tree. We are passionate about the art of Bonsai and want to pass along some of the basic information and necessities required to be successful at bonsai.

Remember! Through failure we find success.

What Is Our Objective?

Our objective is simple - We want to pass along our knowledge and passion of Bonsai to those who truly want to learn and to those who want to become better care takers of their beautiful trees. We want to provide a solidified foundation for all who may want to purchase a bonsai tree, but are uncertain on how to go about taking care of one. We want to offer the information needed, so you can feel confident when initially joining the art of Bonsai. Again, experience will be the greatest teacher when it comes to taking care of your tree, but we offer information pertaining to the following topics:

  • The Variety of Species Found Within Bonsai
  • Understanding a Bonsai's Horticulture
  • How to Properly Care For Your Bonsai
  • Styling Tips to Create a Bonsai Which is Unique to You
  • The History of Bonsai
