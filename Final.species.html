Bonsai Species

Bonsai trees come in a variety of species. Each Bonsai species is met with its own specific needs to properly care for it, so it is vital to execute a proper amount of homework to truly understand the species which you may be interested and gaining the appropriate knowledge to ensure that you have all the tools and resources available to properly take care of your Bonsai tree. Below are the three main types of Bonsai species, along with their respective subclasses:

Broadleaf Evergreen Deciduous Pines and Conifers
Ficus Japanese Maple Juniper
Fukien Tea Chinese Elm Pines
Dwarf Jade Cherry Spruce
Azalea Wisteria Redwoods
Bougainvillea Oak Bald Cypress

The list above shows the various types of Bonsai trees and from which category each type of Bonsai falls in. No one species is exactly the same, so it is extemely important to educate yourself on each species type. The care demands of one species, may be completely different than another species type.
