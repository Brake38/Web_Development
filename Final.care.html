Care and Maintenance

The care and attentiveness for Bonsai trees is much different than that of other household plants. Various Bonsai species will need its own respective soil. Soils contain many different mineral mixtures, so purchasing the proper kind for your Bonsai is important to its growth. Repotting every two years and replenishing the Bonsai with fresh soil will keep your Bonsai happy and content.

Watering the Bonsai tree must be executed with care as well. Each species demands various watering routines. Some species may wane due to over-watering, others may begin to show signs of weakness if too little water is given. Root rot is a common disease which a Bonsai tree may obtain if watered too much. The extended period of moisture and saturation will make the roots soggy and oxygen will be hard to obtain which could end in devastating results. Watering must be precise so again, be sure to do your homework!
