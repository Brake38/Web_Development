Stylistic Options

Styling your Bonsai is the most enjoyable aspect of learning bonsai. There are a plethora of styling options and there is no wrong way of modifiying your small tree. Some of the main styling techniques consist of wiring and pruning the Bonsai tree. With these type of techniques, a Bonsai tree has the ability to grow how you see fit. Branches can be redirected to grow in various angles and foliage can be trimmed to maintain an attractive shaped canopy.

This is where the most fun of gardening a Bonsai tree will lie, but again, not all species can be styled the same way. Most species will allow a heavy amount of pruning, but only during the late Spring/early Summer time once new growth has matured. Executing a hard pruning session could leave your bonsai tree in shock which the tree may never recover from.
